# Credit Policy

Applicants are segmented into Low, Medium, High, and Very High risk bands using bureau score, repayment history, income stability, and existing leverage.

Approval is preferred for applicants with bureau score above 700, stable income, low utilization, and no major delinquency in the last 12 months.

Manual review is required when bureau score is between 620 and 700, enquiries in the last 3 months are high, or active loan count exceeds 4.

Auto-decline is recommended when bureau score is below 580, delinquency in the last 12 months is greater than 1, or income-to-EMI capacity is weak.

Risk teams must monitor approval-rate movements by channel, employment type, city tier, risk band, and product.
