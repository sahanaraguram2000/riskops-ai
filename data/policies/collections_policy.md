# Collections Policy

Loans are tracked using days past due, also called DPD.

0 DPD means current. 1-30 DPD is early delinquency. 31-60 DPD is moderate delinquency. 61-90 DPD is severe delinquency. 90+ DPD is default-risk critical.

Collection calls should prioritize 30+ DPD accounts with high outstanding principal, repeat delinquency, and failed promise-to-pay outcomes.

Portfolio monitoring should include roll-forward and roll-back movement across DPD buckets.
